About ZananTech
ZananTech is a digital movement that helps Afghan women access tech education. Through courses, mentorship, and community, we open doors to a brighter future.

Why ZananTech?

Online Coding Courses

Free programming lessons for women in rural and urban areas of Afghanistan.

Community Support

Join a network of mentors, developers, and learners from across the country.

Access to Resources

Laptops, internet packages, and eBooks provided to committed learners.
